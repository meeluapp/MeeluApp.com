Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2025

1.0 Introduction

At Meelu, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

2.0 Information We Collect

Meelu is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and store sensitive information only on your device:

2.1 Information Stored on Your Device: Exchange API keys and credentials are stored securely on your device using Apple's Keychain technology. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2.2 Data We Collect:

  • Product Interaction: We collect data about how you interact with the app, including features you use and actions you take.
  • Other Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about your usage patterns, including how frequently you use the app and which features you access.
  • Crash Data: We collect information when the app crashes to help identify and fix issues.
  • Performance Data: We collect metrics about app performance on your device to optimize the user experience.

None of the data we collect is linked to your identity or used for tracking purposes across different apps and websites.

3.0 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To operate and maintain the core features of our app.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and improve its features and functionality based on aggregated usage patterns.

4.0 Your Cryptocurrency Data

Meelu is designed to access your cryptocurrency exchange accounts while maintaining your privacy:

  • Exchange API keys are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Keychain technology
  • Your private keys never leave your device
  • Your portfolio data is processed locally on your device

5.0 Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted during transmission using secure HTTPS protocols
  • We regularly review our data collection practices to minimize data exposure

6.0 Third-Party Services

Our app connects to cryptocurrency exchanges through their official APIs. While using our app, you are also subject to the privacy policies of these exchanges. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the exchanges you connect with.

7.0 Your Rights

You have the right to Opt out of future data collection by uninstalling the app

8.0 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

9.0 Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us
